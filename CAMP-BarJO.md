# Bar JO Ranch — Horseback Riding Camp, near Cochrane

## Horse & Pony Adventure Camp Ages 5–7 *(Ages 5–7 | Mixed)* ⚠️ Far

**For her:** A real ranch adventure in the Rocky Mountain foothills — riding horses and ponies, learning how to care for them, and spending the week in the wide-open countryside near Cochrane. Calgary bus pickup available daily.

**For you:** Dedicated ages 5–7 horse camp on 320 acres of foothills. Separate curriculum from the older kids camp. Daily Calgary bus pickup from Central Memorial High School (5111 21 St SW) — not West Hillhurst, but SW Calgary. Hours 10am–3pm at the ranch (⚠️ ends before 3:30pm preference — factor in bus travel).
