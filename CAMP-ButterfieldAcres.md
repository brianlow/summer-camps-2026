# Butterfield Acres — Farm Day Camp, Calgary

## Farm Summer Day Camp *(Ages 4–14 | Mixed)*

**For her:** A real farm day — caring for animals, wagon rides, and outdoor adventures on a working farm near Calgary. Takes home a farm T-shirt and a memory photo with one of the farm animals. Activities vary by group interest and ability.

**For you:** Hands-on farm camp focused on building love and respect for animals, self-confidence, and outdoor exploration. Not a structured sports or arts camp — more free-range farm day. Before-camp care included free from 8:30am. Very limited weeks (only 2 bus weeks in 2026).

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Outdoor (farm setting)
- **Hours:** 9:30am–3:30pm for ages 6–8 ✅ (meets 3:30pm end-time rule exactly)
- **Before camp care:** Free from 8:30am ✅
- **After camp care:** Available (contact farm for details)
- **Price:** ⚠️ Not published — contact farm directly: Office@ButterfieldAcres.com | 403-239-0638
- **What's included:** Farm T-shirt, animal memory photo, all activities
- **Refund policy:** Refund only if camp cancelled due to low registration; substituting another child is allowed if camper can't attend
