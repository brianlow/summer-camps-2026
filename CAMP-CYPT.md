# Calgary Young People's Theatre (CYPT) — Summer Camps

## Full-Day Drama Camp *(Ages 6–12 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Spend mornings learning drama skills — acting, improvisation, stage combat — and afternoons building your own play from scratch, including writing the story, building characters, and performing it for families at the end of the week.

**For you:** Full professional theatre training for kids, led by professional theatre actors, directors, and playwrights. Multiple Calgary locations. Excellent for imaginative, introverted kids who love stories and creative expression. No swimming.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Indoor (drama studio)
- **Hours:** 9:00am–4:00pm, Mon–Fri (full week)
- **Before/After care:** Pre-care 8:00–9:00am ($30/week or $10/session) | Post-care 4:00–5:30pm ($30/week or $10/session)
- **Lunch program:** $65/week (snacks + meals) at select sites
- **Price:** $350/week
- **Locations (multiple — see below):**
  - **North:** Unitarian Church of Calgary, 1703–1 St NW — ~8 min from West Hillhurst ✅ (very close)
  - **South (Riverbend):** Riverbend Community Association, 19 Rivervalley Dr SE — ~35 min
  - **West:** SCA Community Association, 277 Strathcona Dr SW — ~15 min
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **More info / Book:** https://cypt.ca/camps/
