# Clip Clop — Horseback Riding Summer Camp, Calgary

## Rookie Rider Camp *(Ages 5–18 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Learn to ride a real horse! Also learns how to groom, halter, lead, and tack up. Off-horse time includes horse-themed games, crafts, and making new friends at the stable. Extended care available all day if needed.

**For you:** Beginner-friendly horseback riding camp with full horse care education — not just riding. Two structured parts: "Horse Play" (games and learning about horses) and "Riding & Horse Handling" (actual riding + horsemanship). Real uniforms: riders wear helmets and riding gear. Outdoor/active. No swimming mentioned. Sibling discounts auto-applied.
