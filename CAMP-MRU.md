## Magical Adventures *(Gr 1–3 | Mixed)* ⭐ Top pick

**For her:** Become a potion-maker, storyteller, and artist in a magical world! Make spell books, craft wands and amulets, paint dragon eggs, run science experiments, and act out stories. Some weeks include pool time.

**For you:** Fantasy-themed arts & crafts, drama games, and simple science. Broad creative camp — ideal for a child who loves crafts, imagination, and storytelling. Includes pool time in weeks 2–3. Also includes climbing wall in week 1.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Both — indoor studio + pool (select weeks) + climbing wall (week 1)

## C.A.T.S. (Computers, Arts, Theatre & Science) *(Gr 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** A new theme each day across four areas — make crafts, perform with puppets, run science experiments, and learn basic computer skills like typing. Some weeks include pool time and climbing wall.

**For you:** Broad STEAM-flavoured camp blending arts, theatre, and light tech. Good variety; suits kids who like a mix of activities rather than a single focus. Includes climbing wall in week 1; pool in weeks 2–3.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Both — indoor activities + pool (select weeks) + climbing wall (week 1)
