# Pinnovate DIY Studio — Summer Art Camp, Calgary

## Summer Art Camp *(Ages 6–14 | Mixed)* ⚠️ Far (SE)

**For her:** 7 or more hands-on art and craft projects every week — painting, mixed media, DIY builds, and creative games with prizes. Two rooms split by age so projects are always age-appropriate. Snack breaks, lunch break, and outdoor movement break each day.

**For you:** Award-winning arts and crafts day camp — Canada's first DIY studio. High project volume (7+ per week). Age-split rooms. Flexible cancellation (1 week notice for full refund). ⚠️ SE Calgary location is a meaningful drive from West Hillhurst.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Primarily indoor + outdoor movement break (weather permitting)
- **Hours:** 8:15am drop-off | 5:00pm pick-up ✅ (long day — flexible for parents)
- **Price:** $375 + GST / week (~$394 after 5% GST)
- **Cancellation:** Free within 1 week of start; $25 fee for date changes (1 free change)
- **Location:** 137 Mahogany Plaza SE (Mahogany Village, behind Sobeys) — ~35 min from West Hillhurst ⚠️ ([map](https://maps.google.com/?q=137+Mahogany+Plaza+SE,+Calgary,+AB))
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Contact:** 403-278-9065 | info@pinnovate.ca
- **More info / Book:** https://pinnovate.ca/camps/
