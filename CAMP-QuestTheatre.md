# Quest Theatre Summer Camps — Calgary

## Drama Camp Ages 6–8 *(Ages 6–8 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Make a play with a small group of kids! Through imaginative games and storytelling, a story and unique characters emerge naturally. Kids build props and costumes (uniforms!), play outside, and perform for families at the end of the week. Shy or enthusiastic — all welcomed.

**For you:** Creative storytelling and drama camp run by professional theatre artists. Very small groups (max 16), two instructors per class. Strong match for an introverted child — low-pressure, play-based, and creative rather than competitive. Includes prop/costume making (arts & crafts). Culminates in a Friday performance for families.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Both — indoor drama studio + outdoor play time daily
- **Hours:** 9:00am–4:00pm, Mon–Fri (full week)
- **Drop-off/pick-up:** 8:30–9:00am drop-off | 4:00–4:30pm pick-up
- **After-care:** Available until 5:00pm, $40/week
- **Price:** $400/week | $365 early bird (through Mar 21, 2026)
- **Location:** cSPACE Marda Loop, 1721 29 Ave SW, Calgary — ~15 min from West Hillhurst ([map](https://maps.google.com/?q=1721+29+Ave+SW,+Calgary,+AB))
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Group size:** Max 16 campers, 2 professional artist instructors per class ✅ (great for introverted kids)
- **Included:** Complimentary camp t-shirt + pizza party
- **Bursaries:** Available — applications open Apr 1, 2026, deadline May 30, 2026
- **More info / Book:** https://www.questtheatre.org/summer-camps | Register at questtheatre.vbotickets.com
