# Rundle College — Summer Camps

Multiple themed camps for Grades 1–3 (ages ~6–8). Camps are run in AM + PM "combo" pairs — you pick an AM and a PM activity for the week. Both halves are included in the weekly price.

## Creative Souls *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Hands-on creative crafts, visual art projects, and mixed-media exploration — outdoors-inspired art that lets her imagination run wild.

**For you:** Art/crafts half of the day; pairs with sport or drama camps for a full themed day.

---

## Theatre Camp *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Drama games, storytelling, character play — building her own little performance with other kids. Right up her alley for a child who loves stories.

**For you:** Skills-building through drama; builds confidence and creativity.

---

## LEGO® Camp *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Building and storytelling with LEGO — mid-tier interest satisfied!

---

## Sport Sizzler *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** A lively mix of different sports and active games — variety keeps it fun and engaging even for kids who aren't die-hard sports fans.

**For you:** Active, skills-building, and social — good for getting outdoors and building confidence. Available as both an AM and PM option.

---

## Basketball & More *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Basketball skills plus additional sports activities — a good intro to team sports in a fun, low-pressure setting.

**For you:** Active, team-sport focused AM session.

---

## Multi-Sport *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Rotating through multiple sports each day — keeps things fresh and active all week.

**For you:** Active AM session covering a range of sports; pairs well with LEGO, Sport Sizzler, or Math & Art Fusion in the PM.

---

## Stampede *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Calgary Stampede-themed activities and fun — a uniquely Calgary experience timed with the Stampede season.

**For you:** Festive, culturally local PM camp; only offered during Week 1 alongside Stampede.

---

## Math & Art Fusion *(Grades 1–3 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Creative projects that blend art-making with math concepts — good for a curious kid who likes making things.

**For you:** STEM-adjacent without being screen-based; creative and hands-on PM option.

---
