# Strathcona Tweedsmuir School (STS) — Summer Camps

> ⚠️ **Location:** ~45 min south of West Hillhurst (Foothills, AB) — backup tier, same distance as Cochrane
> ⚠️ **Hours and pricing not published online** — confirm before booking

STS Summer Camps are open to all (no need to be an STS student). Set on a large private school campus in Foothills County with extensive indoor and outdoor facilities. 6 weeks of programming July–August.
