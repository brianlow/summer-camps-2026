# Camp STEAM Canada — Calgary

## Camp STEAM *(Age 6–13 | Mixed)*

**For her:** A week of hands-on science, technology, engineering, art, and math projects — themed around Minecraft or LEGO each week. Each week has a different theme so she can do multiple weeks without repeating. Includes a special "impact activity" each week — like coding a message to the International Space Station or NASA-designed astronaut challenges.

**For you:** STEAM-focused day camp (not a sports or outdoor camp). Indoor programming. Four different themes across the summer (Minecraft: Space Expedition, LEGO: Theme Park Fun, Minecraft: Mission to Mars, LEGO: Forging the Future), each offered twice, so she could attend up to 8 non-repeating weeks. Location is Hillhurst United Church — very close to West Hillhurst. 1:7 counsellor-to-camper ratio. All staff have Vulnerable Sector Police Checks; at least one First Aid–certified staff on site at all times.
