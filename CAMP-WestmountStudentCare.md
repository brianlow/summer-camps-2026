# Westmount Student Care — Summer Fun Day Camp

**Provider:** Student Care Calgary (studentcarecalgary.com)
**Two locations:** Calgary Arts Academy (Knob Hill) and Jennie Elliott School

Both programs are full-day summer day camps run by the same provider, with themed weeks, daily arts & crafts, and regular field trips. Each location runs its own weekly themes independently.

---

## Summer Fun Day Camp — CAA *(Grade 1–6 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Themed craft weeks every day — standouts include Storybook Week (Alice in Wonderland tea party, Harry Potter wands, Little Prince paper foxes, Wizard of Oz tornados!), Pirate Party Week (pipe cleaner mermaids, treasure maps, treasure chests), and Medieval Times Week (DIY crowns, rocking unicorns, dragon egg dodgeball). Field trips to Calaway Park, Heritage Park, Calgary Farmyard, and more.

**For you:** Full-day supervision with structured themes, daily crafts, science experiments, games, and weekly field trips. More of a general day camp than a specialty program — flexible week-by-week booking at a lower price point than specialty camps. Closes after Aug 20 (8 weeks total).

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Mix — indoor crafts and activities daily with outdoor field trips
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri, 7:00am–6:00pm
- **Price:** $290/week (Grade 1–6); $120 for Jul 2–3 only; $240 for Aug 4–7 week (4-day); $890 for full month
- **Location:** Calgary Arts Academy — Knob Hill Campus, 2036 20 Ave SW — ~15 min from West Hillhurst ([map](https://maps.google.com/?q=2036+20+Ave+SW+Calgary+AB))
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Registration Deadline:** May 1, 2026 ⚠️
