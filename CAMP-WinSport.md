# WinSport — Summer Day Camps

> ✅ **Close:** Canada Olympic Park, 88 Canada Olympic Rd SW — ~15 min from West Hillhurst
> Free before care (8:00am) and after care (to 5:00pm) included

Summer day camps at Canada Olympic Park set on the Paskapoo Slopes. Two relevant programs for ages 4–8: outdoor nature exploration and multi-sport play. 9 weeks of programming from late June through August.

- **Register:** https://www.winsport.ca/lessons-camps-and-clubs/camp-finder/
- **More info:** https://www.winsport.ca/lessons-camps-and-clubs/camp-finder/wilderness-explorers/

---

## Wilderness Explorers *(Ages 4–8 | Mixed)*

**For her:** Explore the outdoors — guided hikes through the Paskapoo Slopes, nature-based crafts, and hands-on discovery. Lots of time outside in a real natural setting near the city.

**For you:** Nature-based outdoor camp with light physical activity. Includes crafts. Good complement to arts/dance weeks — gets her outside. Free before/after care included.

- **Indoor/Outdoor:** Outdoor (with indoor backup)
- **Hours:** 9:00am–4:00pm Mon–Fri (before care from 8:00am; after care to 5:00pm — both free)
- **Price:** $370/week (5-day); $296/week (4-day holiday weeks)
- **Location:** 88 Canada Olympic Rd SW, Calgary — ~15 min from West Hillhurst ✅ ([map](https://maps.google.com/?q=88+Canada+Olympic+Rd+SW,+Calgary,+AB))
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **More info / Book:** https://www.winsport.ca/lessons-camps-and-clubs/camp-finder/wilderness-explorers/

## Summer Sampler *(Ages 6–8 group | Mixed)*

**For her:** A different activity every day — field games, mini golf, gaga ball, loose parts play, and nature hikes on the Paskapoo Slopes.

**For you:** Multi-sport/activity general camp. Good fallback if arts/dance camps are full. Same location and hours as Wilderness Explorers. Free before/after care.
