### SAIT
- Source: https://www.sait.ca/youth-programs/summer-camps/programs/summer-camp-programs
- Registration portal: https://saitsummercamps.campbrainregistration.com
- Contact: 403-210-5650 | youth.programs@sait.ca
- All Grade 2-3 camps: $400/week, 9:00am–4:00pm, Mon–Fri
- Free before/after care included — no registration needed
- Aug 4–7 is 4-day week (Civic Holiday Aug 3)
- No swimming in any SAIT Grade 2-3 camp
- All indoors at Stan Grad Centre except Great Outdoors (offsite parks)
